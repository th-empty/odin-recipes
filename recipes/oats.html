Oats

Description

Overnight oats are a no-cook, healthy breakfast made by soaking oats in liquid overnight. They're customizable, easy to prepare, and ready to eat straight from the fridge the next morning.

Ingredients

  • Rolled oats
  • Milk or plant milk
  • Chia seeds (optional)
  • Yogurt (optional)
  • Honey or maple syrup
  • Vanilla extract
  • Fruit (e.g., berries, banana)
  • Nuts or nut butter
